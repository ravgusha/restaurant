About us

The Flavors and products typical of Sicily are revived in the restaurants managed by Philip Guardione.

With “Mazzo” you can choose from 3 excellent locations in New York and Ibiza, which evoke sun and sea and where the imagination and creativity of a Sicilian chef is capable of amazing you.

Our team
Master Chef
The success story of “Mazzo” is, without doubt, the story of the chef Philip Guardione, a true Sicilian raised in the shadow of Mount Etna and, due to this, a creative wizard in the kitchen.
As a child, with great enthusiasm and evident talent, he helped his grandmother prepare the food for all the important holidays – Christmas, Easter and Sundays. It was her who passed on the secrets of true Sicilian cooking to a very young Philip.

Specialties
Strawberry ice-cream
We have an old fashioned strawberry ice cream recipe that makes it the perfect spring or summer dessert.
There is just something about home-made strawberry ice cream: it's the taste of blue skies, of sun on your shoulders; an idealised memory of summer in perfect culinary form.
Specialties
Panna Cotta
We will fill the air with the delicious aroma of fresh vanilla and you won't be able to wait to dive into this creamy panna cotta.
Panna cotta (Italian for "cooked cream") is an Italian dessert of sweetened cream thickened with gelatin and molded. The cream may be aromatized with coffee, vanilla, or other flavorings.
Specialties
Profiterolis
Profiteroles are a hazard. It is way too easy to inhale an indecent amount in one sitting.
Crispy hollow choux pastry balls filled with custard or cream then drizzled with chocolate, these are a terrific small-bite dessert option for gatherings.
